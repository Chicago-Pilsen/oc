Our Professional Oil Change Process

At Fernando's Meticulous Auto Repair, we take pride in providing thorough, expert oil change services that extend the life of your vehicle and maintain its optimal performance. When you visit us for an oil change, here's our comprehensive process:

1. Initial Vehicle Assessment

Our certified technicians begin by checking your current mileage and service history to ensure we're providing the appropriate service for your vehicle's specific needs. We'll also verify the manufacturer's recommended oil type and filter specifications for your particular make and model.

2. Vehicle Preparation

We properly position your vehicle on our service lift to gain safe access to the undercarriage. This ensures both safety and complete access to all necessary components.

3. Draining the Old Oil

After the engine has cooled to a safe temperature, we:

  • Remove the oil fill cap to allow proper drainage
  • Position a professional-grade collection container beneath your vehicle
  • Carefully remove the drain plug to allow old oil to flow out completely
  • Allow sufficient time for thorough drainage, ensuring removal of contaminants

4. Oil Filter Replacement

While the oil drains, we:

  • Locate and remove your vehicle's old oil filter using specialized tools
  • Clean the filter mounting surface to ensure a proper seal
  • Apply a small amount of fresh oil to the gasket of the new filter
  • Install a premium-quality replacement filter to manufacturer specifications

5. Drain Plug Replacement

We carefully inspect the drain plug and washer for wear, replacing them if necessary, then properly reinstall and torque the drain plug to specifications to prevent leaks or thread damage.

6. Adding Premium New Oil

We:

  • Add the precise amount of high-quality oil recommended for your specific vehicle
  • Use only oil that meets or exceeds manufacturer specifications
  • Carefully measure the quantity to ensure proper engine lubrication

7. Comprehensive Inspection

While servicing your vehicle, our technicians conduct a multi-point inspection including:

  • Checking all fluid levels
  • Inspecting air and cabin filters
  • Examining belts and hoses for wear or damage
  • Checking tire pressure and condition
  • Testing battery performance
  • Inspecting wiper blades for effectiveness

8. Final Quality Checks

Before completing the service, we:

  • Replace the oil fill cap securely
  • Start the engine and check for proper oil pressure
  • Inspect carefully for any leaks around the filter or drain plug
  • Verify the oil level using the dipstick
  • Reset your vehicle's oil life monitoring system (if equipped)

9. Service Record and Reminders

We provide:

  • A detailed record of the service performed
  • A windshield sticker with the date and mileage for your next service
  • Digital service records that help us remind you when future maintenance is due

Why Our Oil Change Service Matters

Regular professional oil changes are crucial for:

  • Extending engine life by reducing wear on critical components
  • Improving fuel efficiency and performance
  • Preventing costly repairs by identifying potential issues early
  • Maintaining your vehicle's warranty requirements
  • Reducing harmful emissions
