Fernando's Complete Auto Oil Change Guide

Step 1: Preparation

  • Park car on level ground
  • Engage parking brake
  • Gather materials:
    • New oil filter
    • Correct amount and type of oil
    • Oil filter wrench
    • Socket set
    • Oil pan
    • Funnel
    • Gloves
    • Rags

Step 2: Warm Up Engine

Run engine for 2-3 minutes to warm oil (makes it drain better)

Warning: Don't let engine get too hot!

Step 3: Lift Vehicle

  • Use car jack to lift vehicle
  • Place jack stands for safety
  • Ensure vehicle is stable

Step 4: Drain Old Oil

  • Locate drain plug under oil pan
  • Position drain pan underneath
  • Remove drain plug carefully
  • Allow oil to drain completely (15-20 minutes)
  • Replace drain plug with new washer
  • Torque to manufacturer specifications

Step 5: Replace Oil Filter

  • Locate old oil filter
  • Remove using oil filter wrench
  • Clean mounting surface
  • Lubricate new filter gasket with fresh oil
  • Install new filter (hand-tight plus 1/4 turn)

Step 6: Add New Oil

  • Locate oil fill cap on engine
  • Add new oil using funnel
  • Check dipstick for proper level
  • Replace oil fill cap

Step 7: Final Checks

  • Start engine
  • Check for leaks
  • Verify oil pressure
  • Check oil level again after shutdown
  • Reset oil life monitor if equipped
Important: Dispose of used oil properly at a recycling center!
